What is Parkinson’s Disease?

Parkinson’s disease is a progressive neurological disorder that affects movement.

Common Symptoms 🏥

  • ⚡ Tremors (shaking hands, fingers, or legs)
  • 🚶‍♂️ Slow movement
  • ⚖️ Balance issues
  • 🗣️ Speech changes
  • 😟 Muscle stiffness

Precautions & Management 🏡

  • 🍎 Eat a balanced diet
  • 🏃‍♂️ Regular exercise
  • 💊 Follow medication plans
  • 🧘 Manage stress
  • 👨‍⚕️ Regular checkups
